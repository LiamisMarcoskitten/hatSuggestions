StreetFit AI — Hat Style Scanner

What Is This Project?

Many online shoppers struggle to find hats that match their personal style. StreetFit AI uses a Teachable Machine image model to detect the type of hat a person is wearing and suggest similar styles.

This project benefits fashion shoppers, streetwear fans, and online stores looking to create personalized shopping experiences.

How to Use

  1. Click Start to enable your webcam.
  2. Wear a hat (or no hat) and face the camera.
  3. The AI will detect your hat if confidence is high enough.
