StreetFit AI — Hat Style Scanner

What Is This Project?

Many people struggle to find hats that match their style when shopping online. StreetFit AI uses a Teachable Machine image model to scan what type of hat a person is wearing and identify it in real time.

This project benefits online shoppers, streetwear fans, and fashion brands that want to offer personalized shopping experiences.

How to Use

  1. Click Start to turn on your webcam.
  2. Wear a baseball cap, bucket hat, cowboy hat, or no hat.
  3. If the model is at least 60% confident, it will identify the hat.
