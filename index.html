HatVision — AI Hat Style Detector

About the Project

HatVision uses a Teachable Machine model to analyze your hat in real time. It detects what type of hat you're wearing — such as a baseball cap, bucket hat, cowboy hat, fedora, or beanie — and then suggests similar styles that you might like.

This tool is useful for shoppers, fashion enthusiasts, and online stores looking to provide personalized accessory recommendations.

How to Use

  • Click Start to enable your webcam.
  • Look at the camera while wearing a hat OR with no hat.
  • The AI will classify your hat and recommend similar styles.
